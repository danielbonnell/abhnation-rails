Introduction
The following is a comprehensive list of character profiles for the Abh that have appeared in the animated version of the Seikai series. Characters are classified by their genetic race, not by their legal status. While Jinto is legally an Abh, he is not a genetic Abh, and thus is not included on this page.

Linn Ssynec Rocr Ïarlucec Dreuc Haïder Ghintec (Jinto)

    • Ghintec is originally from a planet called Martine. When he was 8 years old, the Abh took control of his home world and his father Rock Linn, the president, sold out to the Abh and became the leader of Martine. Since Ghintec was Rock's son, he was next in line for Rock's position as Nobleman. Ghintec was sent of to the planet Derctuh to learn Baronh and Abh customs before traveling to the capitol to attend a military academy and become a Supply Officer aboard the Basrogrh. Ghintec has served aboard the Basrogrh since then. When the war broke out, his home world joined the side of the United Mankind, but was later recaptured by the Abh. Ghintec chartered a treaty between Martine and the Abh, which would give the people control over the government. In doing so, Ghintec sacrificed his powers and his ability to ever return home without renouncing his title and Abh citizenship. Ghintec is about 6 feet tall, has brown eyes, and is a shy person. He is very attracted to Lamhirh but is too sheepish to admit it. This is evident throughout the anime and novels.


    Ku Dorin (Ku Dorin)

    • Ku Dorin is Ghintec's best friend from the planet Derctuh. Ghintec and Ku Dorin played together on the same team. Not much else is known about Ku Dorin. It is custom on planet Derctuh to call a person by their last name, then first name. Thus, Dorin is his first name, and Ku is his last name. Nothing else is known about Ku Dorin.


    Inspector Entrnh (Inspector Entrnh)

    • Entrnh is the head officer of the Lun Bignhr (Luna Bigga) Police department and was supposed to arrest Lamhirh and Ghintec for a hover car theft. Entrnh supports the Abh over the United Mankind on the grounds that the Abh do not interfere with planetary affairs like the United Mankind does. He is also an avid smoker. No other information is available at this time.


    Minn (Minn)

    • Minn is a member of an Clasbul Anti-Empire group which seeks a starship and independence from the Empire. He owns a villa in the mountains outside Lun Bignhr (Luna Bigga) city, which is stocked with illegal guns and other explosive devices. No other information is available at this time.


    Samsonn Borgh Tiruser Tirusec (Samson)

    • Samsonn is the chief engineer aboard the Basrogrh. He is originally from a planet called Midgrat, which willingly joined the empire and was renamed to Dacumh. His people had a choice to join the United Mankind, but when their strange eating habits were brought up they decided it would be best to join the empire instead. The scar on his face is from a previous battle. He often likes to tease Ghintec about his relationship with Lamhirh but Ghintec takes such teasing fairly well. Samsonn aspires to retire from the Star Forces and return to his home planet to run a small farm.


    Sernaic (Seelnay)

    • Sernaic is one of Baron Feubdach's many vassals. After helping Ghintec and Lamhirh escape from the Baron's manor, she is accepted as a vassal of the imperial family. Later she begins a ship repair business with some of the other former vassals. No other information is available at this time.


    Rock Linn (Rock Linn)

    • Rock Linn is the father of Ghintec and former president, and later Earl of Martine. Although his deal with the Abh was good, his people consider him a traitor and he is later executed when Martine joins the United Mankind. After the empire retakes Martine, his son Ghintec charters a treaty giving executive authority back to the people. Ghintec is re-instated as the leader but has no real power over his people under this new treaty.


    Marca (Marca)

    • Marca is the leader of the Clasbul Anti-Empire group who seeks independence. When the group discovers Ghintec and Lamhirh, she acts as a hotel room service person and assists in their evasion and escape from the United Mankind forces. No other information is available at this time.


    Daswan (Daswan)

    • Daswan is another member of the Clasbul Anti-Empire group. He is a tall and quiet man. No other information is available at this time.


    The Undertaker (The Undertaker)

    • Another member of the Clasbul Anti-Empire group. He is in charge of a funeral center on the outskirts of Lun Bignhr city. He helps Lamhirh and Ghintec escape by launching them into orbit inside a coffin atop a large missile.


    Bill (Bill)

    • Another member of the Clasbul Anti-Empire group, his nickname at home is "Speeder Bill". No other information is available at this time.


    Cyte (Cyte)

    • Cyte is the commander of the United Mankind forces stationed on the planet Clasbul and is using Entrnh to catch Lamhirh and Ghintec. His job later becomes a matter of personal greed. His father was a member of a nation, which was defeated by the empire 120 years ago (Imperial Year 833). His father went under gene manipulation, which is why he ages slower than normal. He is approximately 50 years of age. No other information is available at this time.


    Atosryac Ssynec Atosr Lymh Raica Feubdash Srgumh (Former Baron)

    • He was the Baron of the Feubdach territory until his son imprisoned him because of a personal hatred toward humans. The Former Baron is a human from land but is Abh in title. After his son was killed in a dual with Lamhirh, he was reinstated as the current Baron of Feubdach. His name is not known at this time. No other information is available at this time.


    Diabhc (Diaho)

    • Diabhc is NOT a human; he is the cat of Ghintec and was a gift from Lamhirh. Diabhc is the offspring of one of Lamhirh's many cats in the imperial gardens, and later in Seikai no Senki III, father's a litter of kittens in the imperial garden. Ghintec keeps him aboard the Basrogrh since he has no other home. There is a myth (from ancient Japan, Earth) that having a cat aboard a ship is good luck.


    Administrative Director Geomaiden (Administrative Director Geomaiden)

    • Geomaiden is the Administrative Director of Robnasser (Lobnass) II Penal Colony. He was in charge of keeping order and in charge of the United Mankind forces in the region. He was killed by Angus when he was captured as a hostage.


    Yuri Dukafu (Yuri Dukafu)

    • Yuri Dukafu is a prisoner from the Central Detention Center of Robnasser (Lobnass) II, who was elected by his cronies to represent the Central Detention Center. When Angus seized his territory, his men joined them to get to the woman in the Western Detention Center so they could mate and have children.


    McKay Angus (McKay Angus)

    • McKay Angus is in charge of the Eastern Detention Center, which houses all the men. He is a tough beefy person who believes that it's the will of God that he should rule Robnasser (Lobnass) II. He leads an armed revolt and conquers the Robnasser (Lobnass) II planetary system, but not before most of the women prisoners and staff escape on Abh transport ships. He is later shooting his rifle in anger, at the last transport to lift off. A tidle wave from the transport kills him.


    Lala Shanga (Lala Shanga)

    • Lala Shanga was elected by the prisoners in the Western Detention Center as their leader. She is a convicted murder and thief and tries to seduce Jinto into letting the women evacuate first. She is evacuated from Robnasser (Lobnass) II with the rest of the women prisoners.
