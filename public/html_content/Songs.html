Introduction

The following is a list of song lyrics from the series. Songs are arranged by series and by the order they appear on the soundtracks. When possible, the original Japanese text will be displayed, otherwise the Romanji for the original Japanese text will be displayed. Some song lyrics were originally in English, thus no Japanese translation is available. For practical purposes, Romaji lyrics will be displayed before Japanese lyrics, given the majority of the visitors to this site do not speak Japanese. If you cannot view the Japanese lyrics, in your browser window go to View -> Character Encoding -> UTF-8. This will enable Japanese character encoding.
